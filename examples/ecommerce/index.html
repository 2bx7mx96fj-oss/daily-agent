Our Story

Designed in Auckland,
Loved Everywhere

Nova Home Co. started in 2018 when founder Emma Whitfield noticed a gap in the market — beautiful, thoughtfully designed furniture that didn't cost a fortune or take six months to arrive. What began as a tiny studio in Ponsonby has grown into one of New Zealand's most loved home goods brands.

Every piece in our collection is chosen by our in-house design team. We work directly with craftspeople across Portugal, Japan, and New Zealand — cutting out the middlemen and putting that saving back into quality and design. No flat-pack nonsense. No compromise.

What We Stand For

Built on Four Beliefs

01
Sustainably Sourced
Every material we use is chosen with the planet in mind. FSC-certified timber, recycled textiles, and zero single-use plastic packaging across our entire range.
02
Fair Trade Always
We visit every one of our suppliers in person. Every artisan we work with is paid fairly, works safe hours, and has full visibility of where our orders go.
03
Built to Last
We design against the throwaway culture. Our pieces are built to live in your home for decades, backed by a 5-year structural warranty as standard.
04
Hassle-Free Returns
Not right for your space? No worries. Return anything within 60 days for a full refund, no questions asked. We'll even arrange the pickup for free.
