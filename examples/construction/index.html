// Client Onboarding Checklist

Initial Brief & Site Visit

💰
Fixed-Price Proposal

Contract & Programme

📱
Client Portal Access

🔐  Secure, real-time access throughout your build
01
Free Initial Consultation
We meet on-site or via video call to understand your vision, budget, and timeline. No commitment required — just an honest conversation.
⏱ 1–2 days to schedule
02
Site Assessment & Feasibility
Our team conducts a full site analysis — soil tests, boundary checks, council requirements — so nothing catches us off guard mid-build.
⏱ 3–5 business days
03
Design, Engineering & Consents
We work with your architect or our preferred designers to finalise plans. We handle council consent submissions and manage the process end-to-end.
⏱ 4–12 weeks (council dependent)
04
Fixed-Price Contract Signed
You receive a comprehensive, itemised contract with a locked price and full programme. No surprises, no scope creep — just clarity.
⏱ Within 5 days of consent approval
05
Construction & Milestone Updates
Build begins. Your dedicated PM sends weekly photo updates, flags any decisions needed, and keeps the programme tight. You're never in the dark.
⏱ Per agreed programme
06
CCC, Handover & Aftercare
We complete all council inspections, obtain your Code Compliance Certificate, walk you through the finished build, and remain available for 12 months post-handover.
✅ 12-month defect liability period
