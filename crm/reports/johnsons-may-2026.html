EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Johnsons Property Consultants continues to book approximately 30 valuations per month through existing organic channels — referrals, walk-ins, and direct enquiries. The Google Ads campaign adds confirmed bookings on top of that baseline.

In May 2026, the campaign delivered 363 clicks at a CTR of 13.12% — up from 215 clicks at 9.13% in April, and running at nearly three times the UK Google Ads average of 4.7%. The optimisation score improved from 74.4% to 90%. 1 confirmed in-person valuation booking was directly attributed to the campaign.

The April report identified a mobile form submission failure on the landing page — form completions were not tracking correctly, causing the final week of April (84 clicks) to produce zero confirmed bookings. This issue persisted through May, explaining the gap between strong click volume and a single confirmed booking. This is a tracking and landing page problem, not a demand problem — the organic 30 valuations/month remained entirely unaffected. The new dedicated landing page (valuations.johnsons-property.com) was launched on 2 June to resolve this directly.

✓ CTR Up 44% MoM — 2.8× UK Benchmark

CTR improved from 9.13% in April to 13.12% in May — a 44% month-on-month increase. Click volume rose 69% (215 → 363). The campaign is running at nearly 3× the UK Google Ads benchmark of 4.7%.

⚠ 1 Booking — Landing Page Explains Gap

One confirmed valuation booking in May. The April mobile form failure persisted through May, meaning 363 clicks converted below potential. New landing page launched 2 June resolves this.

ℹ Budget Capping Growth — 20% Impression Share

Campaign flagged as budget-limited. April impression share was 20.26% vs 30–50% local agent typical. Rightmove and Zoopla are absent from valuation-intent auctions — more budget goes directly into unchallenged traffic.

7. Recommendations & June 2026 Targets

 Immediate

  • Test the new landing page (valuations.johnsons-property.com) end-to-end on iOS and Android — confirm form submissions arrive in Johnsons' inbox and trigger Google conversion tracking correctly.
  • Add call conversion tracking — phone bookings from ad clicks are currently invisible in the data.
  • Add audience observation layers: "In-market: Property for sale" and "Moving soon" — builds targeting data without restricting reach.

 Short Term — Next 2–4 Weeks

  • Apply -20% bid modifier on Saturdays and Sundays (April: Saturday £9.98/conv. vs Thursday £3.63/conv.). Redirect saved spend to Thu–Tue windows.
  • Apply +15% location bid uplift for WR10/Pershore (April: 47.06% conversion rate vs 27.56% for WR11).
  • Apply -20% tablet bid adjustment (confirmed negative device signal from April data).
  • Launch dedicated branded campaign at £3–5/day for johnsons terms. CPCs under £0.50 expected, freeing ~£45–50/month of main campaign budget for prospecting.
  • Monitor "property selling house" replacement keywords — ensure new valuation-intent terms are performing to expected CPCs.

 Medium Term — Late June 2026

  • Budget review once clean conversion data is available from the new landing page. Model £18/day and £25/day scenarios — each additional £5/day (~£150/month) at current efficiency adds an estimated 2–3 incremental bookings above the organic baseline.
  • Increase daily budget — Google projects +10% optimisation improvement. Impression share at 20.26% in April vs 30–50% local agent typical — significant headroom available with no major competitor pressure.
